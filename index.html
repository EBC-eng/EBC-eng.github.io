01

Planetary Gearbox

A parametric planetary gearbox designed as a drive component for a 1-DOF foot prosthesis project. Delivers a 6:1 torque increase through a fully mate-driven assembly — geometry updates automatically when gear parameters change. Built with 2 display states and 3 configurations. Modelled in SOLIDWORKS, animated via Motion Study, compiled and exported through SOLIDWORKS Visualize, final output rendered in Blender.

02

Wheels & Battery

A pair of personal Blender projects focused on material development and rendering technique. The wheels were later printed on a Stratasys J850 multi-material PolyJet printer. Both models were produced as promotional material for the Engineering Society. Completed in 3 days as a focused Blender learning sprint.

03

TF2 Teleporter

An early assembly project completed in early 2023, as a SOLIDWORKS Visualize learning exercise and assembly practice. First use of advanced SOLIDWORKS assembly tools. Included here as a baseline — the render quality has held up better than expected. Everything past this point was built on the foundation learned from this project. Personal Recreation for study purposes
